# Text References — Commercial / Marketing / Branding Copy

1. **Mailchimp Content Style Guide** — https://styleguide.mailchimp.com/  
   A comprehensive, brand‑tested voice & tone guide with practical examples for headings, CTAs, errors, and inclusive language—great baseline for web copy.

2. **GOV.UK Style Guide** — https://www.gov.uk/guidance/style-guide  
   Plain‑English standards used at national scale; sharp rules for clarity, capitalization, dates, numbers, and accessibility—ideal to de‑jargonize pages.

3. **Microsoft Writing Style Guide** — https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/style-guide/welcome/  
   Product‑grade guidance on voice (“warm, relaxed, crisp”), bias‑free/inclusive language, and global content—useful for SaaS and enterprise sites.

4. **Shopify Polaris — Content** — https://polaris.shopify.com/content  
   Opinionated content patterns for commerce (voice & tone, naming, error messages, alt text). Handy for product pages, onboarding, and microcopy.

5. **Atlassian Design System — Content** — https://atlassian.design/content  
   Voice & tone principles with concrete grammar and style mechanics; strong reference for friendly, B2B‑ready product and marketing copy.

6. **Apple Human Interface Guidelines — Writing** — https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/writing  
   Emphasis on concise, action‑oriented, helpful text; clear do/don’t examples that translate well to headings, labels, and help copy.

7. **Nielsen Norman Group — UX Writing Study Guide** — https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ux-writing-study-guide/  
   Evidence‑based primers and research on scannability, microcopy, and task‑focused language—good to justify copy decisions with data.

8. **Think with Google — Performance Marketing Best Practices** — https://www.thinkwithgoogle.com/future-of-marketing/creativity/performance-marketing-best-practices/  
   Up‑to‑date marketing playbooks and examples that inform benefit‑led messaging, creative choices, and proof points.

9. **Copyhackers — Website Copywriting 101** — https://copyhackers.com/2022/06/website-copywriting/  
   Practical frameworks, page‑by‑page advice, and conversion copy techniques (headlines, value props, objections, pricing).

10. **Marketing Examples (Harry Dry)** — https://marketingexamples.com/copywriting  
    A curated swipe file of real‑world copy and breakdowns—fast inspiration for headlines, hooks, social proof, and CTA phrasing.
